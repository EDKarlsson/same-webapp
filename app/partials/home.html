About the Student Chapter

In August 2014, a group of students at Saddleback College began the process of creating a S.A.M.E. Student Chapter. They elected a transitional team who will create a charter, constitution, and by-laws for this organization. They will then apply for affiliated club status with the Saddleback College ASG.

For more information about S.A.M.E., you can visit the National S.A.M.E. website or the local Orange County Chapter website.
